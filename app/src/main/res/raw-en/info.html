Purpose:

The objective of this app is to serve as a tracking list of items that are going to be completed.

Its original purpose was to help as a grocery list, with the ability to remove what you already have in your cart, focusing on the items that are still left to find in the store.

As the purpose of this app could be implemented in more than one way, I developed it as a general item-tracking list, with an easy way to clear the selected items.

How does it work?

Sections of the app

"List/s" screen:

In this section, you will find all the created lists.

Here you can see all the content related to each list.

· To add an element, fill in the fields and press the "Add" button to insert a new list (you will be redirected to the new empty list content).

· To access an existing element, tap once on the desired list to open it.

· To delete an element, slide it horizontally.

· To edit an element, hold the item pressed and fill in the new fields in the popup window.

Selected list screen:

Here you can see all the content related to the selected list.

· To add an element, fill in the fields and press the "Add" button to insert a new item (if no quantity is indicated, it will be set to 1).

· To delete an element, slide it horizontally.

· To edit an element, hold the item pressed and fill in the new fields in the popup window.

Information screen:

A simple guide on how to use the app and its purpose.

Where you are right now.

Menu bar:

Simple menu with two icons:

· Icon with an "i" inside a circle: opens the information screen.

· Icon with a down arrow inside a circle: opens the general list screen.
